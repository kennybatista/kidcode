Why Should I Donate?

At Code Pups, we believe coding skills are essential to success in our technology-immersed world. As with learning any other language, studying computer programming at an early age will be incredibly beneficial to future fluency and achievement. We fundraise so that New York City children can be afforded the ability to take afterschool classes through our outreach program, have a personal laptop to refine coding skills, and attend our many coding events and classes.
